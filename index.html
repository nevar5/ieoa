Mission Statement

We, the members of the IEOA, will strive to promote the principles of superior workmanship, rate equity, and value. Further, we resolve to pursue an attitude of community involvement through donated projects for the purpose of returning a benefit to the local area in which we live and work.

Annual Truck Light Convoy and Food Drive

We are disappointed that we can't brighten the convoy route this year with our lights and sounds and wave at you but we want to do our part and keep our community safe. It's with sadness that we announced the cancellation of that part of this year's event. That is the fun part of our event, but the really important part is the food drive. We have collected thousands of pounds of food and thousands of dollars in donations over the past 22 years to help support our local food banks at this time of year. The year of 2020 hasn't been easy and we know the need is greater than ever. The IEOA Members hope you will join us and consider supporting one of these local food banks with a cash donation. Please add "IEOA Truck Convoy" in the dedication box to your donation so we know you're thinking of us also and we really appreciate your support!

Click on the charity of your choice, every $20 will help make 2020 a bit better and provide hope to those that need it most.



There are a number of Drive Through Food Drive events being planned for this year - stay tuned for more information!
We extend our grateful THANK YOU to everyone who has joined us at a Truck Light Convoy and Food Drive in the past and look forward to coming together again on Saturday December 4, 2021 for the next Convoy!

Sooke Minor Fastball Association

On June 23rd the IEOA brought in bobcats, a stone slinger, gravel trucks and material to help with the revitalization of the Art Morris Park in Sooke.

Sooke Minor Fastball is a volunteer based association whose primary goal is to give all children the opportunity to participate in the sport of Fastball.

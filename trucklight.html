Annual Truck Light Convoy and Food Drive

Saturday, December 3, 2016

THANK YOU TO OUR EVENT SPONSORS


The 18th Annual Truck Light Convoy and Food Drive was a great success and comments such as it was "The Brightest Convoy Yet!" and "The participants have gone above and beyond again" have been received. Despite the very wet evening there were hundreds of umbrellas and thousands of people all the way along the route. The Convoy was full and all 80 trucks were dressed in their finest and brightest lights with excited drivers and passengers waving the whole way.

We collected 5800 lbs of food thanks to all the businesses and volunteers who donated their time to support the local food banks, as well as $2418 in cash donations to help fill in the gaps and make the holidays brighter for many people. Thank you to all our sponsors, participants, volunteers and the public for keeping our event going, we look forward to doing it all again on Saturday December 2, 2017!

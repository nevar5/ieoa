Pearce Crecent Play Area

Once again the IEOA has completed another very successful community project. Thank you to the following companies who helped with this project. Your support helped the residents of Pearce Crescent, and the surrounding area, develop a play area for children to exercise and socialize in a safe, fun and friendly environment.

Over the years IEOA members have completed numerous volunteer projects. The IEOA gives us the opportunity to introduce ourselves to the community, and through our donated projects, return a benefit to the local area in which we live and work.
